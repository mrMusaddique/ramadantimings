RAMADAN DUA's

(ANEES GANRAL STORE SILLOD)

  • ramadan dua for (fasting)

    Dua (Prayer) at the Beginning of the Fast

    وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَّوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ

    Transliteration :Wa bisawmi ghadinn nawaiytu min shahri ramadan

    Translate :I intend to keep the fast for tomorrow in the month of Ramadan

  • ramadan dua for (Ifter Dua)

    Dua (Prayer) at the End of the Fast

    اللَّهُمَّ اِنِّى لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ امنْتُ وَعَليْكَ تَوَكّلتُ وَ عَلى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ

    Transliteration :Allahumma inni laka sumtu wa bika aamantu wa alayka tawakkaltu wa ala rizq-ika-aftartu

    Translate :O Allah! I fasted for You and I believe in You and I put my trust in You and I break my fast with Your sustenance

  • First 10 days of Mercy

    رَبِّ اغْفِرْ وَارْحَمْ وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الرَّاحِمِينَ

    Transliteration :Allahumma arhamni Ya Arham-mar-Rahimeen

    Translate :Oh Allah have mercy upon me, O Most Mercifu

  • Second 10 days of Forgiveness

    رَبَّنا فَاغْفِرْ لَناَ ذُنُوبَنَا وَكَفِّرعَنّا سَيِّـٔاَتِنَا وَتَوَفَّنا مَعَ الأَبْرَارِ

    Transliteration :Rabbana faghfirlana zunoobanaa wa kaffir anna sayyi aatinaa wa tawaffanaa ma alabraar

    Translate :Our Lord! Forgive us our sins and remit from us our evil deeds and make us die with the righteous

  • Third 10 days of seek of Refuge with Allah from Hellfire

    اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي

    Transliteration :Allahumma innaka `Afuwwun TuHibbul `Afwa Fa`fu `Annii

    Translate :O Allah, you are the Forgiver, You love to forgive, so forgive me
